Support

Thanks for playing Pet Link Up. This page provides basic support information for the current release of the app.

Game Features

  • Single-player puzzle gameplay
  • Five unlockable stages
  • Device-language aware English and Simplified Chinese support
  • Optional background music toggle

Troubleshooting

  • If the app language looks outdated, fully close and reopen the app after changing device language.
  • If audio does not start immediately on mobile, tap the screen once to enable audio playback.
  • If app progress looks incorrect, reinstalling the app may reset locally stored progress.

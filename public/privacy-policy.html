Pet Link Up · Privacy Policy

Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 15, 2026

Pet Link Up is a single-player puzzle game. We designed the game to work without creating an account and without uploading gameplay data to our servers.

Information We Collect

We do not collect personal information such as your name, email address, phone number, or precise location through the game itself.

The game stores basic progress data on your device only, such as unlocked levels, stars, coins, and background music preferences. This information is not sent to us.

How We Use Information

Local save data is used only to keep your game progress and settings available on your device.

Analytics, Ads, and Tracking

  • We do not use third-party analytics SDKs in the current version of the app.
  • We do not show third-party advertisements in the current version of the app.
  • We do not track you across apps or websites owned by other companies.

Children's Privacy

Pet Link Up is designed to be family-friendly. Because we do not knowingly collect personal information through the app, there is no intentional collection of children's personal data.

Data Retention

Progress and settings remain on your device until you remove the app or clear the app's local data.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy if the app introduces online services, analytics, ads, or account features in the future. If that happens, the updated policy will be posted at this URL.

Contact

For support requests, please visit the Pet Link Up Support page.

Before submitting to the App Store, review this page again if you add analytics, advertising, account login, cloud save, push notifications, or in-app purchases.

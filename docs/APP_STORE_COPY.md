## English (U.S.)

### App Name
Pet Link Up

### Subtitle
Cute match puzzle with quick rounds

### Promotional Text
Match adorable pet and fruit tiles, clear the board before time runs out, and enjoy a cozy puzzle game designed for quick play sessions.

### Keywords
match,puzzle,pet,link game,casual,brain,tiles,family,cute,offline

### Description
Pet Link Up is a bright and cozy tile-matching puzzle game made for fast, relaxing play sessions.

Match two identical icons and clear the board before time runs out. Each connection path can use no more than two turns, so every move is a small logic challenge.

Why you'll enjoy Pet Link Up:

- Cute pet and fruit themed icons
- Easy to pick up and play
- Five handcrafted stages with increasing difficulty
- Quick rounds that fit short breaks
- Simple local progress saving
- English and Simplified Chinese interface support
- Optional background music toggle

How to play:

- Tap two matching icons
- The icons disappear if they can be connected with no more than two turns
- Clear every icon before the timer reaches zero
- Finish faster to earn a better score

Pet Link Up is perfect for players who enjoy light puzzle games, family-friendly visuals, and short sessions on the go.
